The character sprite is a modified version of a spritesheet found at OpenGameArt.org:

https://opengameart.org/content/2d-rpg-character-walk-spritesheet

Other visuals are created with Krita:

https://github.com/KDE/krita

The `level.map` file consists of hexadecimal values referring to the index of the tiles in `environment.png`

00 01 02 03
04 05 06 07
08 09 0a 0b
0c 0d 0e 0f
